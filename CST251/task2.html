Cal State University Monterey Bay
Monte Rey is the official CSUMB mascot. Monte lives within the Associated Students and loves attending campus events & fostering school pride.

Monte Rey Facts

  • Born in 1994 along with the founding of CSUMB
  • Spotted at most home CSUMB sporting events entertaining fans along the sidelines and in the stands
  • Monte resides in Kelp Bed on the campus of CSUMB
  • Is a Sea Otters (Enhydra lutris), which are distinct from river otters and other carnivorous mammals in the subfamily Lutrinae.
